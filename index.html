About

Mediflow Manager is an efficient system to meet the all the requirements of Medical Inventory with a web based digital infrastructure. It allows an easy mangement procedure to manage the complete medical Stock from a small tablet to heavy machinery. Features include Restocking, Editing Stock, Viewing Stock and Selling from the stock.
